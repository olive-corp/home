Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to mobile applications developed by Olive Co., Ltd., including Oopstime and Nonospam.

These applications do not collect personal data. All user data is stored locally on the user’s device.

Nonospam may use Apple’s Message Filter Extension to identify and filter spam messages. All message filtering happens on the user’s device and no SMS message content is transmitted to external servers.

  • Collected data: None
